A new, cleaner, simpler Web View

Have you ever stumbled upon an article, but skipped because the layout of the website sucked? Or made it difficult for you to read the text?

We want to solve this problem by providing a platform which loads the same readable content from the website or blog, and displays it in a much cleaner, simpler, and readable format. Without all the unnecessary stuff.

All you need to do is add reedme.in just before the url of the website in your address bar and bam!

All you need to do is, open the article in your favorite browser.

lifehacker.com/top-10-ways-to-beat-a-bad-mo....

And add reedme.in in the address bar, just before the URL

reedme.in/lifehacker.com/top-10-ways-to-beat-a-bad-mo....
